# 📅 Devlog #001.Week 1 - The Birth of LevelCraft

**Date: April 27, 2026**

The project has been active for over a week, marking a successful strategic shift from "cloning a classic" to "original development." Here is the summary of our progress:

#### 1. Branding & Identity
* **Official Name:** Officially named **LevelCraft** (conceived and recommended by AI).
* **Domain Name:** Registered **levelcraft.gg**. Regardless of the final outcome, I want this project to have the "official feel" of a serious endeavor from day one.

#### 2. Technical & Artistic Pivot
* **Strategic Leap:** The focus has evolved from a simple *Super Mario Bros* level clone to building the core mechanics of a "Level Editor" inspired by *Super Mario Maker*.
* **Visual Style:** Moved away from traditional pixel art, settling on a **Sci-fi Monochrome** aesthetic.
* **AI Collaboration:** With AI's help, all art assets have been regenerated and replaced, including the new robot protagonist and futuristic environmental elements.

#### 3. Milestones Reached
* **World 1 Prototype:** A basic playable version (World 1) using the new assets and Godot engine is now live.
* **Workflow Validation:** Successfully verified that the "Zero Hand-Written Code" workflow is highly efficient, even when handling complex logic like physics collisions and movement "feel."

#### 4. What's Next
* **Level Editor Prototype:** The current levels are still clones of the old game. I'm done tweaking things manually for now. Let's get to work on the level editor, and I'll rebuild the levels once the tool is up and running.
